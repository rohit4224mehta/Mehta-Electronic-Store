About Us




How was Mehta Electronic Store started?

Mehta Electronic Store, located in Rajkot, Gujarat, India, was founded by Rohit Mehta on September 25, 2023. With a vision to provide the latest in consumer electronics and cutting-edge technology, Rohit Mehta identified a growing demand for high-quality, affordable electronic devices in the local market. His passion for technology and business led him to create a store that offers a wide variety of products, from smartphones and laptops to home appliances and accessories. The journey of Mehta Electronic Store began with a focus on customer satisfaction, quality service, and innovative products. Rohit Mehta's goal was to create a place where customers could find trusted brands and expert guidance, ensuring they made the right choices for their technological needs. With a strong emphasis on after-sales service and a customer-first approach, Mehta Electronic Store quickly gained popularity in Rajkot and its surrounding areas. In just a short time, the store has established itself as a reliable destination for electronics, thanks to its comprehensive product range, competitive pricing, and commitment to excellence. Mehta Electronic Store continues to expand, driven by a dedication to staying ahead of technological trends and meeting the needs of an ever-growing customer base.
